The Stardust Programming Language

What is Stardust?

Stardust is a language born out of frustration with other languages. After falling into the black holes of other languages for years, Stardust was finally born.

Status: WIP.

Currently, things are still in very early development; the syntax and goals of the language are still in flux. The GitHub issue tracker is being used like sticky notes - they'll be your best bet for understanding the direction the language is going.

Didn't this used to be called Cosmos?

Yes, but months after the name Cosmos had been decided on, someone else started a language also called Cosmos. Since their language was more developed, they kept the name.
